CTC participation leaderboard

Proposed ranking method

Each team carries a total number of points from month to month. This total is adjusted according to the number of boats the team has floated in the current month.

Let x be the number of boats floated by a team. Then:

  • • If x is greater than zero, the team gains floor(100·log(x+1)) points. ♦ What does the formula mean?
  • • If x is zero, the team loses 20% of its total.

Teams are ranked according to their total. ♦ How are ties broken?

Ties for first place are broken according to the following rules:

  • • A team that has never chosen a challenge is preferred.
  • • Otherwise, a team that chose its last challenge longer ago is preferred.

  • • If there is still a tie, the winner is chosen randomly.

Note: Due to the precision of the scoring formula, ties for first place occur infrequently. For the past nine years of CTC data, there are no such ties with this formula.

The team in first place wins the right to select the next challenge, and its total is reset to zero.
