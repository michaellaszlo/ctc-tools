CTC participation leaderboard

Ranking formula: logarithmic accumulation and slow decay

Each team carries a running total from month to month. This total is adjusted according to the number of boats the team has floated in the current month.

Let x be the number of boats floated by a team. Then:

  • • If x is greater than zero, the team gains floor(100*log(x+1)) points. [make an info box for the formula]
  • • If x is zero, the team loses 10% of its total from the previous month.

Teams are ranked according to their running total. [put the following in an info box] If several teams are tied for first place, the tie is broken as follows:

  • • A team that has never chosen a challenge is preferred.
  • • Failing that, a team that chose its last challenge longer ago is preferred.

  • • If there is still a tie, the winner is chosen randomly.

The team in first place wins the right to select the next challenge, and its running total is reset to zero.
