Dec 2012 - A flying ten reps of 1' with 1' rest
This challenge was chosen by the Forum Flyers


It's a simple, unrestricted straight forward set of intervals.

First rep must be from a stopped flywheel, subsequent reps can be a static start or rolling start.


The January challenge will be chosen by SUB7. See the C2 forum thread for details.
