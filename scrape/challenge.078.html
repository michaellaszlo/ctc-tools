Jan 2012 - The SUB7 Seven Seven Sevens
This challenge was chosen by SUB7

You have to row seven intervals of 777m and record the total time for 5439m.

You have seven minutes of resting time to divide between your 777m repetitions


So, for example, rowing 777m with 70 seconds between each of the seven reps (no rest after the last) is allowed.
You could do 777m, 50" rest, 777m, 50" rest, 777m, 70" rest, 777m, 70" rest,
777m, 90" rest, 777m, 90" rest and finish with 777m and that's within the rules too.


First rep must be from a stopped flywheel, subsequent reps can be a static start or rolling start, it's your choice.
