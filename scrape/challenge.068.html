Mar 2011 - The Swedish Classic Flat-pack Back Breaker Challenge
This challenge was chosen by ÄIRC team.

Row 4 x any distance reps of your choosing but the four reps must add up 4000metres.
3 x 2minute rest intervals in between the reps.

Enter the total time for 4K, do NOT include the six minutes of resting.

So, for example, 1300m, 2'R, 1300m, 2'R, 1300m 2'R and a final 100m would fit those rules. So would a simple 4 x 1K 2'R.

First interval MUST be from a standing start, 2nd, 3rd and 4th can be standing or rolling starts.
