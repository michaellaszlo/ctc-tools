Feb 2012 - Carole McNally Memorial Intervals
This challenge was chosen by Team Oarsome - lets all remember Carole McNally.

You have to row five intervals of 1402m with 2 minutes and twenty seconds rest between each rep.

Record the total time for 7010m. Do not include rest time.


First rep must be from a stopped flywheel, subsequent reps can be a static start or rolling start, it's your choice. There are no rate restrictions.
