Feb 2008 - Magnificent McNally's Memorial 1K
In memory of Carole McNally who sadly lost her battle against cancer a year ago today, you are invited to row your heart out over 1Km.

Carole took part in every CTC challenge from its inception until she was no longer able, was a core personality on the indoor rowing scene, World Record holder and great friend to many. So, do your best and make this a fitting tribute to an inspirational lady.

Carole, you are sorely missed by us all and forever in our thoughts.
