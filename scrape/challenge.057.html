May 2010 - The E.R.G Team: 500,400,300,200,100
This challenge was chosen by the E.R.G. Team.
It's 500m, 400m, 300m, 200m, 100m with 90 seconds interval rest between each rep.


PM3/PM4 users should program this as 5 Variable Intervals and the monitor will give you the total time for the 1500m.


PM2 users will need a pencil and paper, a stopwatch to time the intervals and some deft fingers to program each repetition as a new session. Getting someone to help may be the best option (as you sweat, breath heavily and struggle to recover).



Please enter the total time for 1500m, don't include the six minutes of rest.
