Sep 2014 - The Joan Van Blom Cancer Awareness row
The CTC for September is a special event.


In November 2013 Joan van Blom was diagnosed with a brain tumour.


Row 7684m from a standing start and record your time.


The 76 is to honour JVB's silver medal in the 1x at the 1976 Montréal Olympics;
the 84 is to honour her silver in the coxed quad at the 1984 Los Angeles Olympics.
Stroke rating is up to you; the only rule is that the row should be continuous.

A special team "Friends of Joan Van Blom" has been created for anyone who doesn't belong to an existing team.

If you have strange desires to pour buckets of ice over your head,
please do so and give a donation to a cancer charity. This part is optional and won't affect your results.

The October challenge will be chosen by The Fitness Matters Team. See the ISS forum thread for details.
