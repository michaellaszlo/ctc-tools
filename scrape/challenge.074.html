Sep 2011 - 30' rowing 5' resting (any way you slice it)
This challenge was chosen by Paddypower
It's simple, you're going to row for thirty minutes and rest for 5 minutes.

You can divide it up any way you like.

Six times five minutes one minute rest is valid,
15' rowing, 3' resting, 10' rowing, 2' resting, 5' rowing is valid.
30' rowing, 5' resting is also valid.

Only record the distance done for your 30 minutes (any way you slice it).
Do NOT record any resting metres.

No restrictions. Standing start, subsequent intervals can be a rolling start.
