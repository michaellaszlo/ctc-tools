Feb 2016 - The RowPro Lucky 7
This challenge was chosen The RowPro Rower Team


Row for seven minutes three times
with five minutes rest between each rep


Start each seven minute row from a standing start.


Record the distance rowed for the slowest of your seven minute rows.
So you can't do a warm-up, a hard 7min and a cool down.


The March challenge will be chosen by Red Line Rowers.
