Jul 2013 - Two minutes @ 20SPM, it's Bananas.

This challenge was chosen by Banana Boat


Row for two minutes at twenty strokes per minute (or less)


Record the total distance for the 2 minutes.
Do not exceed 20SPM as the average for your two minutes.


Just for Phil Morris.
This challenge must be done on a single machine with one person rowing, starting from a stationary flywheel.


The August challenge will be chosen by SUB7 IRC
