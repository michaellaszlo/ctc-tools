Oct 2014 - The Sting in the Tail
This challenge was chosen by The Fitness Matters Team.

It's a modified version of the evil and hated Rugby Test

Row 1000m from a standing start, 3 minutes rest,
row 750m, 2 minutes 30 seconds rest,
row 500m, 2 minutes rest,
row 250m, 1 minute 30 seconds rest,
And finally row 750m.


Record your total time for 3250m.
Logging a time for a straight 3250m is against the spirit of the challenge.

Unrestricted rate. Rolling start allowed other than for the 1000m.

The November challenge will be chosen by the Age Without Limits team

See the ISS forum thread for details.
